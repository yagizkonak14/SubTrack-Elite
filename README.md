SubTrack Elite

A simple subscription tracker that respects your privacy.

What is this?

SubTrack Elite is a small web app I built to track subscriptions without sending any data to servers.

Everything runs directly in your browser.

No accounts. No backend. No cloud.

Why I made this?

I noticed most subscription apps require you to:

create an account
trust them with your financial data
accept tracking and analytics

I didn’t like that.

So I built something minimal where:

your data stays on your own device.

Features:
Track your subscriptions
See monthly and yearly totals
Simple category breakdown
Clean and fast interface
Data saved in your browser (localStorage)

How it works:

There’s no backend.

When you add something:

it gets saved in your browser
the UI updates instantly
charts are generated on the fly

That’s it.



Privacy:

No tracking.

No cookies.

No data collection.

Everything stays local.

Notes:
If you clear your browser data, your entries will be gone
Data is not shared between devices
